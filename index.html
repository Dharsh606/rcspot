Frequently Asked Questions

What is the delivery time?

We offer fast and reliable delivery! Standard delivery takes 3-7 business days. Express delivery (2-3 business days) is available for an additional charge. All orders are processed within 24 hours of confirmation.

Do you provide warranty?

Yes! All our RC cars come with a comprehensive 6-month warranty covering manufacturing defects. We also offer extended warranty options. Our customer support team is always ready to assist with any issues.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unused items in original packaging. Items must be in perfect condition. Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the item is defective.

What batteries do the RC cars use?

Most of our RC cars come with rechargeable lithium-ion batteries included. Charging time is typically 2-3 hours. Battery life varies by model but generally provides 15-30 minutes of continuous use.

Do you provide spare parts?

Yes! We stock a wide range of spare parts for all our models. Common parts like wheels, batteries, and controllers are readily available. Contact our support team for specific part inquiries.
